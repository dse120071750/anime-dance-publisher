Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: AURA is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

Uploaded Images

Photos you upload for AI processing

Generated Content

Videos and images created by our AI

Social Tokens

Authentication tokens for publishing

Usage Data

Analytics and interaction metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To generate AI dance videos from your uploaded images
  • Quality Improvement: To enhance our AI models and user experience
  • Social Publishing: To post content to connected social media accounts at your request
  • Communication: To send updates and respond to inquiries
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices:

  • Images are stored in Google Cloud Storage with encryption at rest
  • API keys and tokens are encrypted and never exposed in client-side code
  • Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed

4. Third-Party Services

AURA integrates with several third-party AI services:

  • Google Gemini 3: For image generation and analysis
  • Kling AI (via FAL): For video motion transfer
  • Minimax: For AI music generation
  • Instagram/TikTok APIs: For social media publishing

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data processing.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data and generated content
  • Revoke social media permissions at any time
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Receive information about how your data is used

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Uploaded Images: 30 days after generation (unless you delete sooner)
  • Generated Videos: Indefinitely (unless deletion requested)
  • Social Tokens: Until you revoke access
  • Usage Data: 12 months for analytics purposes

7. Children's Privacy

AURA is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the Service or via email.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: Project Repository
  • Community Discord channels
